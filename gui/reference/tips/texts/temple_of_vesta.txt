THE TEMPLE OF VESTA
Like standard Temples, use this structure to heal nearby units and buildings.
Available for free to the Imperial Romans at the start of a skirmish match and cannot be rebuilt if destroyed.
For the Republican Romans, available in Village Phase and automatically researches the Town Phase technology instantly upon completion. Build Limit: 1.
“Eternal Fire” aura: All nearby Roman buildings +25% loyalty, but requires the “College of Vestal Virgins” technology.
