WONDERS AND EMPIRE PHASE (IV)
Automatically boost your faction up to the Empire Phase (IV) by building a Wonder.
Cost is 1000 stone, 1000 glory, and 1000 seconds.
Unlocks strong technologies, special features, and special units for many factions.
“A Wonder for the Ages” aura: Population cap +10% to maximum as long as the Wonder exists, and for every Wonder under the player's control.
