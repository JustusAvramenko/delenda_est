WAR ELEPHANTS
Powerful combat units available in City Phase to: Carthage, Epirus, Kush, Macedon, Maurya, Persia, Egypt, and Seleucia.
Train at the Elephant Stable for a significant cost.
Effective against Soldiers on the battlefield, but vulnerable to Javelin Infantry and Sword Infantry to a lesser extent. Flaming Pigs can be used to frighten elephants and reduce their effectiveness.
“Terror” aura: Elephants reduce nearby enemy units' attack rate and movement speeds by −25%.
